Amana Printing: Professional Design, Printing & Digital Marketing Services Since 2005

Amana Printing, based in New York, is a trusted leader in professional design, printing, and digital marketing services. Since 2005, we have proudly served a wide range of clients—from small businesses to established brands—by delivering high-quality creative solutions that elevate their presence and help them grow. With two decades of experience, our team combines creativity, precision, and industry expertise to produce designs that reflect each client’s identity and prints that meet the highest standards of quality. We specialize in everything from business cards, brochures, and company profiles to modern social media marketing and effective digital advertising strategies. At Amana Printing, we believe in building long-term relationships with our clients. Our commitment to excellence, fast turnaround, and attention to detail has earned us the trust of hundreds of clients over the years. We transform ideas into powerful visuals—helping your business stand out, attract customers, and succeed

Our Vision

Sola Rosette is based in the heart of Amman, Jordan. More than a cosmetics company, we are a journey into the world of beauty and care. Our vision is to harness the wonders of nature to create exceptional skincare, haircare, and beauty products that inspire confidence and elegance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, nature-inspired beauty solutions that combine effectiveness with luxury. We are committed to innovation, sustainability, and providing products that enhance natural beauty while caring for skin and hair.

Our Goals

We aim to become a trusted beauty brand locally and internationally by offering premium products, maintaining the highest quality standards, and continuously evolving to meet the needs of modern beauty enthusiasts.
